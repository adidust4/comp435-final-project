National Urbanicity and Outcomes

Over the last 100+ years, the United States have become increasingly urbanized. It is estimated that in 2021, 83% of the United State's population lives in some kind of urban area. With so many people living in an urban area, what does the remaining 17% experience? What do those in the most urban areas experience? We decided to look at this in terms of education, considered to be the great equalizer. Is education effected by district urbanicity? How might this great equalizer fail in consideration of locale type?
